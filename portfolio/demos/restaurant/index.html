The room

Enough atmosphere to desire, enough clarity to book.

A restaurant page should make the next step obvious without turning into a PDF menu. The concierge fills the gaps — quietly, the way a good host would.

i

Reservations

Date, party size, time preference and contact — collected in a sentence or two, not a form marathon.

ii

Dietary notes

Common allergies answered with confidence; anything uncertain is passed to the kitchen, never guessed.

iii

Private dining

Occasion, guest count, date and budget captured for the events team to follow up personally.
